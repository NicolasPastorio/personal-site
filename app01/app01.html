Welcome to the Beloved Date app's Privacy Policy. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This policy describes what information our app collects and how that information is used and protected.

By using the Beloved Date app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Data We Collect

Our app is designed to function independently, without the need for external servers. All information you provide is stored exclusively on your own device. We collect the following types of data:

  • Event Card Data
    • Name: The name of the person or event you wish to record (e.g., "John's Birthday," "Wedding Anniversary").
    • Date: The date associated with the event (e.g., date of birth or date of celebration).
    • Event Type: The card category, such as "Birthday" or "Special Date".
    • Image (Optional): An image that you choose from your device's gallery to associate with a card. The app requests permission to access the gallery only for this purpose.
  • Application Settings
    • Preferred Time for Notifications: The time of day you select to receive event reminders.
    • Theme Preference: The choice between light or dark theme for the application interface.
    • Delete all: A button to delete all cards and notifications.

We do NOT collect personally identifiable information, such as your name, email, address, or location data.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used exclusively for the operation of the application on your device. The purposes are:

  • To display your events: Names, dates, and images are used to create and display event cards within the app.
  • To Calculate Dates and Ages: Dates are used to calculate a person's age or the time elapsed since a special event, displaying this information for you.
  • To display local notifications: The event date and your preferred time are used to schedule local notifications on your device, ensuring you are reminded of important dates.
  • To personalize your experience: Theme preferences are used to adjust the visual appearance of the application.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Service Providers

Our app uses Google AdMob to display ads, which helps us keep the app free for all users. AdMob is an advertising service provided by Google Inc.

When you use our app, Google AdMob and its partners may collect and process data to provide you with personalized ads. The data collected may include, but is not limited to:

  • Google Advertising Identifier (GAID): A unique ID for advertising that can be reset or disabled in your Android device settings.
  • IP Address: Used to determine your approximate location (country/city) and for security purposes.
  • Ad Interaction Data: Information about the ads you see and how you interact with them (e.g., clicks).
  • Technical Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and language settings.

Google uses this data to:

  • Show ads that are relevant to your interests.
  • Measuring the effectiveness of ads.
  • Limit the number of times you see the same ad.

We do not share the data you enter into the app (such as names and event dates) directly with Google AdMob. The data collection performed by AdMob is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

For more information about how Google uses your data, and to understand your privacy and control options, please visit the "How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services" page: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites and for "Privacy Information": https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can manage your ad preferences and turn off personalization through Google Ads Settings and your mobile device settings.

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

"The information you enter directly into the app (names, dates, images, and theme/notification settings) is stored exclusively in a local database on your device. We do not have access to this data.

However, as detailed in the 'Advertising' section, our advertising partner (Google AdMob) collects usage and device data, which is processed on Google's servers in accordance with its own privacy policies".

5. Data Security

The security of your data depends on the security of your own device. Because the information resides locally, it is protected by the security measures you use on your device (such as passwords, biometrics, etc.). We use standard development practices to ensure that the application securely stores data within its isolated operating system environment.

6. Notifications

The app requests permission to send notifications for the sole purpose of reminding you of birthdays and special dates you've registered. Notifications are scheduled locally and do not require an internet connection to function.

7. Your Rights and Control Over Your Data

You have complete control over your data. At any time, you can:

  • Delete Data Individually: Delete specific cards, which will permanently remove the associated data from the local database.
  • Delete All Data: Use the "Delete All Cards" function in the app settings. This action is irreversible and will delete all event cards and related scheduled notifications.
  • Uninstalling the App: Uninstalling the app from your device will permanently remove all local database and saved settings.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We recommend that you review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
